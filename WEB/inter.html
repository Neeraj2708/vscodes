Knowing INTERNET

The Internet is a network of computers spanning the globe. This communication structure is a connecting people all around the world. A global web of computers, the Internet allows individuals to communicate with each other. The Internet allows individuals to communicate with each other. The Internet is often referred to as a network of networks or the World Wide Web. It is a means of connecting a computer to any other computer anywhere in the world via dedicated routers and servers.

WHAT IS INTERNET ?

Internet refers to the global computer network providing a variety of information and communication facilities, consisting of interconnected networks using standardized communication protocols.

HISTORY OF INTERNET

APRANET

IN 1957, the Internet began as APRAnet, a U.S. Department Of Defense project to create a nationwide computer network to connect all the computers in the Department Of Defense to a central computer.





Vinton Cerf

IN 1972,Vinton Cerf was appointed as the head of APRANET. Later he was known as the father of Internet.He allowed other countries too come under this network
